Pokéval Description and Functionality

A quick tool for checking your opponent's type, strengths and weaknesses

Evaluation by Pokémon

You type in the name of the Pokémon you're battling against
(Either in English or German)
and then press ENTER or click 'confirm'
to get shown the types in the effectiveness-category you selected.

Remember that certain abilities have a major impact on the immunity of
certain attacks of a type.
(For example: Pokémon with 'levitate' are immune to almost all Ground attacks)
These might be added in a future update.


Evaluation by Type

You type in the Mono- or Dualtype you'd like to check (in English)
and then press ENTER or click 'confirm'.
Typing in the same type twice is considered an invalid entry.

This is basically the effectiveness chart
in a more manageable way. It is useful when you
are struggling to remember which types a particular type
is effective or not effective against.
